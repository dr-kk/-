Dr. K. Kumar received his M.Tech and Ph.D degrees from the department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), VNIT Nagpur, Maharashtra, India. Presently, he is working as an Assistant Professor at Computer Engineering Department, National Institute of Technology Kurukshetra, Haryana, India. Prior to this assignment, Dr. Kumar spent about 8.5 years at NIT Uttarakhand as a faculty member where, he served as a coordinator of Campus Wide Networking from July 2016 to June 2019, as a Head of the department from March 2019 to February 2022 and as a Coordinator of Career Counselling and Placement from March 2022 to November 2022. He was also the chairman, coordinator or special invitee in the various department and institute level committees including the DFB, DPGC, DUGC, Purchase and Library Committee, Senate, ACoFAR, etc. He has more than 10 years of experience in teaching, administration, research and Industry.

Currently, he is working on a DST-sponsored project titled "Secret Sharing Scheme Based Technology for Multimedia Security over Cloud" with a financial support of Rs. 40 Lac for duration of 03 years. He supervised 08 M. Tech. scholars and leading 04 Ph. D. scholars. He authored 100+ technical research articles in reputed international conferences and journals, including IEEE and ACM Transactions with a citation of 1000+ with H-index 20. His research article was selected for the best student paper award in the IAPR endorsed International conference PReMI, ISI Kolkata, India, in December 2017. Dr. Kumar received the Best Faculty Award 2022 for his significant contributions in teaching, research and administration during the academic year 2021-22 at NIT Uttarakhand. He is Senior Member of IEEE USA, Life member of ISTE, IETE and IUIAPR, Member of IET and ACM. Dr. Kumar is serving as an Editor at the IETE Journal of Research (SCI, IF: 2.333) and Senior Editor at the Artificial Intelligence Evolution, Guest Editor at special session of 29th ICONIP’22, Guest Editor at special session of 04th ICDLAIR’22, Publicity Chair at 9th UPCON’2022. His research interests include machine learning, deep learning, video processing, NLP, Multimedia analysis, and Cloud computing.
